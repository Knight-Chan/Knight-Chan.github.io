Biography

I am a M.Phil. student supervised by Prof. Sai Weng SIN and Prof. Mingqiang Guo in the Institute of Microelectronics, University of Macau. IME is also the State Key Laboratory of Anology and Mixed-Signal VLSI (AMSV), where I start to work on High-speed data converters research (especially SAR ADC) in 2022.

Previously, I received my bachelor's degrees by the School of Electronic Science and Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, where I systematically studied microelectronic technology from 2018 to 2022.

Research Interests

  • High-speed data converters
  • Background calibration techniques for ADCs
  • Analog and mixed-signal CMOS integrated circuits
